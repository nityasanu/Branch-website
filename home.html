About Department of Information Science and Engineering

The Information Science and Engineering department is committed to continuously improve the quality of education by enhancing the knowledge of student and staff members. The department of Information Science and Engineering with highly qualified staff is well equipped with centralized laboratories having the latest configurations and software tools. The department presents a unique opportunity to study the exciting field with guidence of quality teachers. The department of Information Science and Engineering has been established in 2001 with an intake of 60 which later enhanced to 120 in 2007. In 2009, a post-graduation programme has been launched in Computer Network Engg with an intake of 18. As of now, the department has produced about 1000+ graduates and about 70+ post graduates.The department is proud to say that three UG students and one PG student have secured ranks at the state level University.The department has conducted 9 National Conferences,01 International Conference and about 14 faculty development programs for the benefit of teaching fraternity in and around Bangalore. The department is a recognized as research center under VTU(2010) wherein about 17 faculty members are working for their PhD. There are totally 30 faculties in the Department, where 3 Professors, 5 Assoc. Prof, 22 Asst. Prof and 10 non-technical staffs headed by Dr. Mohan H.S

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality technical education in the field of information technology and to strive for excellence in the education by developing and sharpening the intellectual and human potential for good industry and community

Our Vision

We envision our department as a catalyst for developing educated, engaged and employable individuals whose collective energy will be the driving force for prosperity and the quality of life in our diverse world
